# Motivating Scenario
## Name
Agents' characteristics

## Description

Actors involved in the description, management or digitisation of cultural heritage objects, such as people and organisations, can be described using a series of features.

If an agent is a person, they can be described in terms of:
- their date of birth;
- their full name;
- their first name;
- their last name;
- their country;
- their language;
- their gender;
- their description;
- their date of death;
- possible links to their authority records.

If an agent is an organisation, it can be described in terms of:
- its starting date;
- its name;
- its country;
- its ending date;
- possible links to its authority records.

## Example 1
